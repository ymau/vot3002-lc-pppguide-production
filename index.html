Overview

What you will achieve

By the end of Lesson 10, you will deliver a confident group presentation of your project plan using clear PPT slides.

Lessons 1-5:
From ideas to slides

draft PPT slides

Lessons 6-10:
From slides to delivery

Focus:
Structuring and delivering a strong group presentation

What you produce:
Polished PPT slides +
an oral group presentation (TA02)
